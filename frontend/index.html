Keep it clean and dry

Before recycling, empty and rinse containers. Food residue can contaminate the recycling process.

Remove non-recyclables

Remove caps, lids, and any non-recyclable parts from containers. Labels are okay, but plastic films or foil should be removed.

Avoid bagged recycling

Do not place recyclables in plastic bags; they often can't be processed and may end up in the trash.
